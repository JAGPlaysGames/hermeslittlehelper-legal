Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-11

X1xJAGx1X Content Studio is a creator workflow tool for JAG Plays Games / X1xJAGx1X. It helps authorised operators connect the creator’s TikTok account, review content workflow information, and manage approved creator operations. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information the app may access

  • Basic TikTok account information returned by TikTok Login Kit, such as open ID, username/display name, avatar, or account identifiers when provided by the API.
  • OAuth authorization data required to keep the authorised creator connection working.
  • Creator-approved workflow records such as selected video filenames, captions, titles, descriptions, hashtags, approval notes, upload status, and post identifiers when those features are enabled.
  • Technical logs needed to verify callback delivery, troubleshoot failed authorization, and comply with platform requirements.

TikTok permissions

For the initial TikTok review, the app is intended to request only:

  • user.info.basic — to identify the authorised TikTok creator account after Login Kit authorization.

Additional TikTok scopes or products will be requested only when the matching user interface, workflow, and review demo are ready.

How information is used

Information is used only to operate the creator workflow: account authorization, showing connected account status, preparing approved content workflow records, troubleshooting integration issues, and complying with TikTok developer requirements.

What the app does not do

  • It does not sell personal information.
  • It does not share TikTok account data with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.
  • It does not scrape TikTok or collect data outside authorised API responses.
  • It does not use TikTok data for profiling, credit scoring, employment decisions, surveillance, or unrelated analytics.
  • It does not post or make external account changes without creator approval in the intended workflow.

Storage and security

OAuth tokens and app configuration are stored only for operating the authorised connection. Access is limited to the creator/operator environment. Logs should avoid printing access tokens, refresh tokens, or client secrets.

Retention and deletion

Authorization data is retained only while the creator wants the app connected. Workflow records may be retained for operational records and troubleshooting. Users can request deletion of locally stored app records using the Data Deletion page.

Revoking access

The creator can revoke TikTok access from TikTok account settings or developer authorization settings. After revocation, the app can no longer use the revoked token to access TikTok APIs.

Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children. It is a creator operations tool for authorised management of creator-owned content.

Contact

For privacy, support, or data deletion requests, use the contact options on the Support page.
