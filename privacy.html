Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-11

JAGPlaysGamesHelper is a private creator operations tool used by JAG Plays Games / X1xJAGx1X to prepare, manage, and publish approved short-form gaming videos to connected creator accounts. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information the app may access

When a creator connects a TikTok account through TikTok Login or another official platform authorization flow, the app may access only the information required for the approved workflow:

  • Basic account information returned by the platform, such as account ID, username, display name, or avatar, when provided by the API.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required to keep the authorized connection working.
  • Creator-approved video files, captions, titles, descriptions, hashtags, scheduling notes, upload status, and post identifiers.
  • API response metadata needed to verify whether an upload or publish action succeeded.

TikTok permissions

For TikTok, the app is intended to request only these scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to identify the authorized TikTok creator account.
  • video.upload — to upload approved videos to the creator’s TikTok publishing workflow where available.
  • video.publish — to publish approved videos where the creator account and TikTok API access permit it.

The app does not request TikTok Data Portability API scopes for this workflow.

How information is used

Information is used only to operate the creator workflow: account authorization, preparing metadata, uploading approved videos, publishing approved videos, recording post IDs or URLs, troubleshooting failed uploads, and complying with platform requirements.

What the app does not do

  • It does not sell personal information.
  • It does not share TikTok account data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • It does not post without creator approval in the intended workflow.
  • It does not use TikTok data for unrelated profiling, credit scoring, employment decisions, or surveillance.
  • It does not request access to private messages, contacts, or data portability exports.

Storage and security

OAuth tokens and app configuration are stored in the creator’s controlled environment for the purpose of maintaining the authorized connection. Access should be limited to the creator/operator. Logs should avoid printing access tokens, refresh tokens, or client secrets.

Retention

Authorization tokens are retained only while the creator wants the app connected. Upload logs, approved captions, and post identifiers may be retained for operational records and troubleshooting. The creator can request deletion of locally stored app records using the instructions on the data deletion page.

Revoking access

The creator can revoke TikTok access from TikTok account settings or developer authorization settings. After revocation, the app can no longer use the revoked token to access the TikTok API. Locally stored tokens should also be deleted from the app environment.

Children’s privacy

JAGPlaysGamesHelper is not directed to children. It is a creator operations tool for managing the creator’s own content publishing workflow.

Contact

For privacy, support, or data deletion requests, use the contact options on the Support page. You can also contact the creator brand through the public JAG Plays Games / X1xJAGx1X social channels listed there.
