HermesLittleHelper Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

HermesLittleHelper is a private creator tool used by JAG Pixel to manage and publish approved short-form gaming content to connected social media accounts.

Information the app may access

When connected to TikTok through TikTok's official authorization flow, the app may access the information permitted by the scopes approved by the account owner. This may include basic profile information and permissions needed to upload or publish video content.

How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate the account, confirm creator settings, and upload or publish content that has been approved by the account owner. The app does not sell personal information.

Tokens and credentials

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's device or server and are used only to communicate with TikTok's official APIs. Tokens are not intentionally shared with third parties.

Content data

The app may process video files, captions, hashtags, schedules, and publishing status logs for JAG Pixel content management. This data is used for creator workflow and publishing records.

Data sharing

Data is shared with TikTok only as required to use TikTok's official APIs and publish approved content. The app does not sell or rent data to advertisers or data brokers.

Data deletion

To request deletion of app-related data or disconnect the app, revoke app access from the connected TikTok account and remove locally stored tokens from the operator's device.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the app or platform requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the JAG Pixel operator through the official JAG Pixel / X1xJAGx1X social channels.
