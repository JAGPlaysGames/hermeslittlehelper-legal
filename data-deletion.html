Data deletion

Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-06-11

JAGPlaysGamesHelper stores only the information needed to operate the creator-approved publishing workflow, such as OAuth tokens, approved upload metadata, post IDs, logs, and configuration records.

Disconnect TikTok access

  1. Open TikTok account settings.
  2. Find security, apps, connected apps, or authorized apps settings.
  3. Remove or revoke access for JAGPlaysGamesHelper.
  4. After revocation, the app cannot use that authorization to access TikTok APIs.

Delete locally stored app data

To delete locally stored JAGPlaysGamesHelper records, remove the app’s TikTok token file and any local upload logs or schedule records associated with the app. Typical records may include:

  • OAuth token files stored in the creator’s controlled app environment.
  • Upload logs containing post IDs, timestamps, and verification statuses.
  • Approved captions, titles, descriptions, hashtags, and scheduled publish notes.

Deletion request

If you need help deleting app data, contact the creator/operator through the public channels on the Support page. Include the account or workflow you want disconnected and the type of records you want deleted. Do not send passwords, client secrets, access tokens, or private keys.

Expected handling

Deletion requests are reviewed and handled as soon as reasonably possible. Once records are deleted, they may not be recoverable. TikTok-side access revocation must be completed through TikTok’s account settings or authorization settings.
