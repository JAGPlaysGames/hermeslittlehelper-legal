Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-06-11

Users can disconnect TikTok authorization and request deletion of locally stored X1xJAGx1X Content Studio records.

Revoke TikTok access

  1. Open TikTok account settings.
  2. Go to the connected apps / security authorization area where TikTok lists authorised apps.
  3. Find the app authorization and revoke access.

After revocation, the app can no longer use the revoked token to access TikTok APIs.

Request deletion of app records

Contact the creator/operator through the channels on the Support page and request deletion of app records. Include the TikTok username or account identifier used with the app, if available, so the operator can locate the relevant records.

What may be deleted

  • Stored OAuth authorization records.
  • Connected account identifiers.
  • Local workflow logs associated with the connected account.
  • Upload or callback troubleshooting logs that are no longer required.
